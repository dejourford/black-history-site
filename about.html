Mission

This site was created because I've noticed over the years that my son wasn't learning much Black History. When I was younger, Black History month was a time in school to be excited for; It was the month where classes did a lot of activites, projects, and watched educational films. But now...they seem to have lost sight of that. So my mission, you ask? My mission is to educate the youth even with the smallest amount of Black History.
